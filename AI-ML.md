## **Language & Speech Services**

- **Amazon Comprehend** – Analyzes text to extract insights like sentiment, key phrases, and language.
    
    _Use cases:_ Sentiment analysis, content classification, compliance monitoring.
    

- **Amazon Polly** – Converts text into natural-sounding speech in multiple languages and voices.
    
    _Use cases:_ Virtual assistants, e-learning, accessibility.
    

- **Amazon Transcribe** – Converts speech to text with features like speaker identification and real-time transcription.
    
    _Use cases:_ Call transcription, sucbtitles, media indexing.
    

- **Amazon Translate** – Translates text between multiple languages in real time or batch.
    
    _Use cases:_ Document translation, multilingual apps.

### **Computer Vision & Search Services**

- **Amazon Kendra** – Intelligent enterprise search using natural language understanding.
    
    _Use cases:_ Smart search, chatbots, internal knowledge bases.
    

- **Amazon Rekognition** – Analyzes images and videos to detect objects, people, text, and activities.
    
    _Use cases:_ Content moderation, identity verification, media analysis.
    

- **Amazon Textract** – Extracts text, forms, and tables from scanned documents.
    
    _Use cases:_ Financial, healthcare, and government document processing.

### **Conversational AI & Personalization**

- **Amazon Lex** – Builds chatbots and voice assistants using text and speech.
    
    _Use cases:_ Virtual assistants, FAQ bots.
    

- **Amazon Personalize** – Creates personalized recommendations using user data.
    
    _Use cases:_ Product, movie, and content recommendations.
